Privacy Policy for Geometry Solver

Effective date: June 25, 2026
Developer: VSG Designs
Contact: vsg.design.dev@gmail.com

Geometry Solver (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is an offline geometry calculator. This policy explains what information is and is not involved when you use the app, with a focus on the advertising and in-app purchase features.

1. The short version

  • The app works offline and does not require an account or collect personal information about you on our side.
  • It shows ads through Google AdMob, which collects limited device and ad-interaction data to deliver and measure ads.
  • It offers one optional one-time purchase, Remove Ads, handled entirely by Google Play Billing.
  • It is intended for users aged 13 and older.

2. Who the app is for

Geometry Solver is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

3. Information the app collects directly

Geometry Solver does not require an account, login, or registration, and it does not ask for, collect, or store any personal information on our servers. The measurements you type are used only on your device to compute results; they are not transmitted to us or stored on any server. A small amount of non-personal preference data (such as your theme, unit, and a record of whether you purchased Remove Ads) is stored only on your device.

4. Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, Google and its advertising partners may collect and process certain information from your device, such as:

  • a device advertising identifier;
  • general device and app information;
  • IP address and approximate (non-precise) location derived from it;
  • ad interaction and usage data.

This data is used to deliver, limit, and measure ads. We do not receive your personal information from this process; it is handled by Google as described in Google’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Advertising policies. Because the app targets users 13 and older, ads may be personalized where you consent and where permitted; you can opt out as described in “Your choices” below.

5. Consent

Where required (for example, in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland), the app uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before personalized ads are shown, and respects your choice. You can review or change your choice at any time, and you can manage ad personalization and reset your advertising identifier through your device settings.

6. In-app purchases

The app offers a single optional Remove Ads purchase (a one-time, non-consumable item). Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details; Google processes the payment and shares only a confirmation that the purchase was completed. Your purchase status is stored on your device and verified with Google Play so it can be restored on the same account.

7. Third-party services

The app relies on the following Google services, which have their own privacy practices:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play services / Google Play Billing (purchases) — https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell your personal information, and the app itself does not share personal information because it does not collect any. Ad-related data is processed by Google and its advertising partners as described above.

9. Your choices

  • Reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings → Privacy / Google → Ads).
  • Where a consent prompt is shown, your choice is respected; you can also manage ad personalization through your device’s Google settings.
  • The one-time Remove Ads purchase disables ads entirely.

10. Data retention and security

We do not retain personal data on our servers because we do not collect any. On-device preferences remain until you clear the app’s data or uninstall it. Any ad- or purchase-related data is retained by Google in accordance with Google’s policies. Information handled by Google is protected under Google’s security practices.

11. International users

The app is available internationally. Information processed by Google (for ads and purchases) may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, in accordance with Google’s policies.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: vsg.design.dev@gmail.com.
