Privacy Policy for Geometry Solver

Effective date: June 19, 2026
Developer: VSG Designs

Geometry Solver (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a geometry calculator. This policy explains what information is involved when you use the app.

1. Information the app collects directly

Geometry Solver is an offline calculator. It does not require an account, login, or registration, and it does not ask for, collect, or store any personal information. The measurements you type are used only on your device to compute results; they are not transmitted to us or stored on any server.

2. Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain information from your device, such as a device advertising identifier, general device and app information, IP address and approximate (non-precise) location, and ad interaction/usage data. This data is used to deliver and measure ads. We do not receive your personal information from this process; it is handled by Google, as described in Google’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Advertising policies.

3. Consent

Where required (for example, in the EEA and UK), the app uses Google’s User Messaging Platform to request your consent before personalized ads are shown, and respects your choice. You can also manage ad personalization and reset your advertising identifier through your device settings, as described in “Your choices” below.

4. Children

Geometry Solver is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us using the details below and we will take appropriate steps.

5. Third-party services

The app uses Google AdMob and Google Play services, which have their own privacy practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell your personal information. The app itself does not share personal information, because it does not collect any. Ad-related data is processed by Google as described above.

7. Your choices

  • You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings → Privacy / Google → Ads).
  • Where a consent prompt is shown (for example, in the EEA and UK), your choice is respected; you can also manage ad personalization through your device’s Google settings.
  • A one-time “Remove ads” purchase (planned) will disable ads entirely.

8. Data retention

The app does not retain personal data on our servers because it does not collect any. Any ad-related data is retained by Google in accordance with Google’s policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: vsg.design.dev@gmail.com.
